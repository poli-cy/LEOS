                        EDIT METADATA - HOWTO
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Open the proposal by clicking on the 'Open' button or on the proposal card 
	in the repository browser. The document will open for editing in the 
	proposal viewer.

2.  The only meta data that can be changed for the moment are :

	- the proposal title . Click in it to edit it.
	- the annexe(s) title(s) . Click in it to edit it.
   
3. For both element, a "V" and a "X" is available to the right of the editing field
   in order to 'Save' or 'Cancel' the change.
