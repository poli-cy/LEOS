                            ADD MILESTONE - HOWTO
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  In the proposal view, on bottom-right, milestone panel appears for document author
    and admin/supports users.

2.  To add a milestone click on '+' icon and 'Add a milestone' pop-up will appear.  

3.  From the combo-box select a title for the milestone or select 'Other' option to add some free text title.

4.  Click on 'Create milestone' button. After a few minutes milestone will be added.

5.  A major version was created for legal text and annexes documents.
